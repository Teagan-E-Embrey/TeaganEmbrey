Line Drawings in Illustrator

Achilles

For this line drawing project, I chose 4 images of animals that I like, including my pet Husky Achilles. This image is from a hiking trip my wife and I had taken him on. This is the image that took me the longest of all of my line drawing works to do just because of how much detail I wanted to put into it.

Original photo by: Teagan Embrey

Wolf Growling

With this image, I had chosen a wolf growling. I had to use the pen tool to outline the image. It took a while to finish all of the strokes. After I was done drawing the lines, I selected the whole set of lines and changed their styles. I am happy with how these turned out.

Original image by: Philipp Pilz

Curious Fox

With this one, I had chosen an image of a fox looking at the camera. I had once again used the pen and curvature tools to trace out and draw the lines to give it the texture and flow of the fur. I am happy with how this one turned out as well.

Original image by: Sunguk Kim

A Husky at Peace

I was looking for a fourth image of another husky when I came across this one in my Unsplash search. I though this husky looked so peaceful in it's environment and I wanted to recreate that here. Again I used the pen and curvature tools to outline and draw the flow of the fur before selecting the line styling.

Original image by: Taylor
