About Shy Wolf

Shy Wolf Sanctuary is this quarters featured sanctuary. Shy Wolf started in 1933, rescuing wildlife that government services, wildlife rehabilitars and zoos were not accepting. They became a 501(c)3 charity in 2001 with their own independant board of directors and volunteer staff. To this day they work to rescue a wide range of wildlife including Wolves, Cougars, Sugar Gliders, and more.
