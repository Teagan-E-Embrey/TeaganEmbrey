Money Donations

Money Donations help us in many ways if you aren't sure how you want to contribute. With money donations, we are able to pay vet bills for animals that have been injured, buy specific items for the animals, and build more enclosures and maintain the ones we have.

Money donations are crucial, especially for animals that get hurt, or develop medical issues. For example, Tewa is likely going to have long term concerns related to injuries he sustained as pup.

Money donations will help us with any current and future issues and health concerns he may run into, as well as any of the other animals may run into. We appreciate all donations.
