About Teagan

Hello everyone, my name is Teagan and I am the founder of this site. Ever since I was little, I have had a fascination with wolves, foxes, and other wild animals. I liked to do research and learn about them constantly. Growing up, i had always wished that I could volunteer at a sanctuary and/or a rescue for wild animals. I never got the chance to unfortunately.

After I joined the Marine Corps, my love for these animals didn't go away. I got to visit some sanctuaries that had various wolf species among other animals like foxes, dingos, etc. It took me a few years to figure out what I wanted to do in terms of a career long term. Eventually I settled on Web Development, and with that, I birthed the idea for this website.

About Us

The idea for this site came from my love for wild animals and my appreciation for the sanctuaries and rescues that help them. When I started learning web development, one of my projects was to create a website, or rebuild one of my choosing. That was when the idea for this site came to mind.

I had finally found a way that I could help the sanctuaries and rescues that I support. It can be difficult for them to get the finances, food, and other resources they need to care for these animals. So, I created this site to help them as much as I can.

This site will be completely non-profit and will represent a different sanctuary every quarter. All money donati0ons made will go straight to that sanctuary, as well as food and toys ordered on this site will ship straight to the sanctuary. I will never hold onto the money as I will never see it. That will go straight into their accounts. I do not hold the toys, going through me, you are purchasing toys from an external site and they will automatically ship to the sanctuary.

We hope that you will continue to support the represented sanctuaries. For questions regarding a specific animals favorite foods or toys, please contact me by clicking here.
