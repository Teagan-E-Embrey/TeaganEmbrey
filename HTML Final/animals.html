Apollo

Apollo came to Shy Wolf as an Emergency Rescure request back in 2021. He was sad at first but greatful for the attention, love, and food he was recieving at Shy Wolf. Despite the fact that Apollo is deaf, he is an Ambasador for the Shy Wolf Sancturay.

Bob Leo

Bob Leo is a bobcat who was brought to Shy Wolf in Febuary of 2021. He had been purchased when he was a kitten, and for 8 years he lived indoors, not really able to get the exercise he needed. Adjusting to the sanctuary was hard, he was depressed and stressed out, often refusing to eat. Through some TLC and vitamin B shots, Bob Leo was able to get back to eating and is finally warming up to the new environment.

Jasper

Jasper came to Shy Wolf in 2015, after recieving a call from a couple that realized they were in over their heads with a wolfdog puppy and expecting a child. Shy Wolf took him in and had a DNA test for the pup and learned that his is 95+% wolf. Jasper hasn't warmed up to the others yet so he still lives alone in his own enclosure. He seems to be happy though and loves his toys.

Anja

Anja is a red fox that Shy Wolf recieved after her previous owners decided to move to a state that doesn't allow foxes as pets. This broke Anja's trust and she hasn't opened up the volunteer's at Shy Wolf. While she hasn't warmed up to her new home, people, and other animals around her, the volunteers at Shy Wolf still do everything they can to take care of her and enrich her life.

Mikey

Mikey was discovered at Shy Wolf at such a young age, that he still had his umbilical cord attached. They guessed he was only two or three days old and took him in, as he likey wouldn't have made it on his own at that young of an age. He lives at the sanctuary, sharing his uniqueness with the residents, visitors, and volunteer staff. When he isn't playing with the other racoons or on the "busy board", he likes to hang out in the hammocks on of the volunteers made for the racoons.
